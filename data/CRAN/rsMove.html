rsMove: Guidelines for the use of Remote Sensing in Movement Ecology

Tools for the guided selection of satellite data and environmental predictors, the combination of remote sensing and animal movement data and the mapping of resource suitabilitz. Based on the paper by Remelgado et al. (2015) <doi:10.1002/rse2.70>.
