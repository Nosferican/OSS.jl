BimodalIndex: The Bimodality Index

Defines the functions used to compute the bimodal index as defined by Wang et al. (2009) <https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2730180/>, <doi:10.4137/CIN.S2846>.
